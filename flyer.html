CoDAIM 2026

Workshop on Co-Design for Agentic and Multimodal AI

March 23, 2026 Co-located with ASPLOS 2026
Co-Design: Application + Algorithm + PL + OS + Network + Compiler + Architecture

About the Workshop

The CoDAIM workshop brings together researchers and practitioners from programming languages, compilers, systems, and computer architecture to explore principled co-design approaches for agentic and multimodal AI systems. Join us for keynotes, contributed talks, and panel discussions on emerging challenges at the intersection of AI systems and architecture.

Topics of Interest

Foundations of generative & agentic AI inference
Cost-, latency-, & energy-optimized inference
System architectures for AI agents
Model compression (quantization, pruning)
Caching, batching, speculative decoding
Hardware-aware optimizations
Efficient inference on GPUs, TPUs, ASICs
AI-driven system optimization
Cross-layer algorithm-compiler-runtime co-design
Real-world deployment case studies
